What am I working on right now?

Maine Boys to Men Internship

To continue to explore my passion of having productive conversations surrounding ending gender-based violence, I decided to pursue an internship with nonprofit organization Maine Boys to Men, based in Falmouth, Maine. I was involved with the organization throughout high school and in my first year of college, and as an intern, I am now involved with them to a greater extent than I ever have been before. Developing a machine learning model for Union College Admissions

Continuing my research project from the summer of 2020, I am working with Union College's Admissions department to develop a machine learning model based on their past application data. The model is built to predict the probability of enrollment for a given applicant provided the information they supplied when applying to the College, how many times they visited campus, etc. These predictions are, in turn, used to predict the incoming class profile to forecast the enrollment and finances of that class. Please see the "Coding Projects" page for more information on this project.

This project will be used as a reference point in the College's 2021 Admissions cycle, and my work this fall is focused on refining the model for use. Specifically, I am continuing to improve the model's predictive performance and preparing an automation system that will run the model and produce a class profile on a weekly basis as the College receives more applicants.
