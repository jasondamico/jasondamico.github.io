Passions

I have a lot of things outside of programming that I am passionate about! Here are some highlights of my non-work life:

Coffee

I have spent two stints working as a barista: one in the summer of 2019 at Coffee By Design in Freeport, Maine and the other in the summer of 2023 at HiFi Donuts and Coffee in Portland, Maine. Through each experience, I have developed a love for coffee and crafting specialty drinks that drives me to explore coffee shops in cities I visit in search of the best espresso drinks. I also have grown to love the atmosphere of coffee shops and find them fun to visit both when I need to get good work done and during my days off. My go-to drink is a cappuccino: to me, it is the perfect combination of steamed milk, foam and espresso.
